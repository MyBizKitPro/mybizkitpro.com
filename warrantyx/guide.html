Step-by-Step Guide

Master Warrantyx in 2 minutes

Five short walkthroughs covering everything from your first scan to email forwarding and tax exports. Built for busy people who'd rather see than read.

1Sign up & first scan 2Scanning a receipt 3Tax tracking & export 4Email forwarding 5Plans & boost packs
1

Create your account

Takes 30 seconds. We'll send a confirmation email — click the link, and you're in.

Step 1

Sign Up

Open warrantyx.mybizkitpro.com and tap Sign Up. Email and password — that's it.

Step 2

Confirm your email

Check your inbox for a confirmation link. The app shows a green check while you wait.

Step 3

You're in

Land in your empty vault. Receipts, warranties, and tax claims will fill in as you scan.

Tip: No confirmation email? Check Spam/Junk and mark it Not Spam — future emails will land in your inbox.
2

Scan your first receipt

Snap it with your camera, upload a file, or do a multi-page scan. AI extracts merchant, total, date, and warranty automatically.

Step 1

Tap Add

Tap the big circle to launch the camera, or pick Upload File / Multi / Manual underneath.

Step 2

Frame the receipt

Lay it flat, point your camera, tap the shutter. No cropping needed — AI handles it.

Step 3

Review extracted data

AI fills in everything. Confirm the details look right and save — it's now in your vault.

Tip: PDF invoices work too. Use Upload File instead of the camera and AI extracts data from the PDF the same way.
3

Tax tracking that survives EOFY

Toggle Tax Deductible on any receipt. At tax time, export everything as a CSV or PDF report — itemised, GST-broken-down, ready for your accountant.

Step 1

Toggle on the receipt

Open any receipt, flip the Tax Deductible switch. GST and ex-tax amounts calculate instantly.

Step 2

See your year-to-date totals

Insights tab shows tax claimable, GST paid, and a category breakdown for the financial year.

Step 3

Export for your accountant

Tap PDF Report or CSV. Get an itemised summary with every receipt, GST, and ex-tax amount.

Tip: The tax note field on each receipt (e.g. "WFH equipment") flows straight through to the PDF report's Purpose column. Future-you will thank present-you.
4

Set up email forwarding

Save digital receipts without lifting a finger. Forward any receipt email to your unique address — Warrantyx scans the attachment and adds it to your vault automatically.

Step 1

Get your forwarding address

Settings → Email Forwarding. Tap Copy. Save it as a contact named "Warrantyx" in your email app.

Step 2

Forward a receipt

When a receipt email arrives, hit Forward and send it to your Warrantyx contact. Done.

Step 3

Get instant confirmation

~30 seconds later you'll get a "1 receipt imported" email and the receipt appears in your vault.

Tip — first time only: The first confirmation email may land in Spam/Junk. Mark it as Not Spam and all future ones go straight to your inbox. Available on the Core and Pro plans.
